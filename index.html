About

My name is Jenice Feliciano also know as Realistic Creators. I'm a London based cinematographer, videographer and photographer. I've also done other professional roles such as producer in film, music production, director and camera operator. I've learned how to edit my own projects for clients.

I cherish that my job allows me to explore me all over the world, to create unique stories and capture memorable moments. This has inspired me to start my own professional videography and production business to put myself out there for clients.

I am a graduate from University of West London, studied BA Film Production within the course, I have worked in television and the film industry which allowed me to engage with the roles that I am Passionate about. To gain further experience I have decided to create realistic creators which is specialised in promotional content, interview and documentary/ journalism, realistic content as well.
